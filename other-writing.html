Auxiliary mathematical ideas

About 90% of what I work on never sees the light of day. Below are some short notes, sketches, and working writeups hosted on HackMD or arXiv. These are intended as in-progress thoughts, reproducible sketches, musings, curiosities, and sometimes dead ends. Since these ideas are not peer reviewed, you should proceed with caution - and charitably please :) If you see something interesting that you could "unstuck" or make a project out of, please reach out. Also, if these ideas below at all lead to progress in your work, please help me by acknowledging my work.
