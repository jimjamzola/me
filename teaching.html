Seminars

Paper Club (student-led)

A student-run reading group where participants present and discuss recent papers across applied analysis, numerical methods, and scientific computing. The club emphasizes student leadership, critical reading, and presentation practice.

Website / schedule: Paper Club (site) · Contact: student organizers (email / Slack; replace with details as needed).

Data-Oriented Mathematical & Statistical Sciences Seminar (DoMSS)

A seminar series focused on data-driven mathematical and statistical methods, interdisciplinary applications, and invited research talks. I co-organize this seminar with Heyrim Cho; we invite speakers across applied math, statistics, and data science.

Schedule and talks: ASU events — DoMSS. · Co-organizer: Heyrim Cho.

If you would like to present to either series or recommend a speaker, email me at jimmie.adriazola@asu.edu.
