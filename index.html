Research Focus

I work on scientific machine learning for dynamical systems mostly coming from Hamiltonian, quantum, and nonlinear wave settings. My research combines analysis, numerical methods, and machine learning to produce models that respect invariants, symmetries, and physical structure. Most of my earlier work involved numerical optimization and optimal control.

New and Active Projects

  • Reduced order models for coherent structures
  • Sparse identification of Lax pairs from symbolic data
  • Neural Tangent Flows for long-time integration
