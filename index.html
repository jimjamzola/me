Research Focus

I work on structure-preserving neural surrogates for partial differential equations, with an emphasis on long-time fidelity, geometric integration, and learning constrained dynamics. My research combines analysis, numerical methods, and machine learning to produce models that respect invariants, symmetries, and physical structure.

This site collects publications, preprints, slides, and project pages with example code and notebooks.
